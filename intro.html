What is Elf?

Elf is a closed SNS for school. It was made for better communication between students and teachers, and furthermore between students. You can create classes, chat with teacher and students, and get closer with teachers. Elf will help you live a better school life. Outcasts, bullies, insiders, outsiders all can get together in Elf. The word Elf itself has no meaning. Originally, the name was Edupla, but it was too long, so we shortened it to Elf.

What is SNS?

A social networking service (also social networking site, or SNS or social media) is an online platform which people use to build social networks or social relations with other people who share similar personal or career interests, activities, backgrounds or real-life connections.

Who use Elf?

Elf is made for students all over the world, and was made by Korean students. Elf's goal is to help all students get close with teachers. Elf is, and will be used all over the world. Elf can also be used in companies and all sorts of groups. We will keep all your talks secret, so don't worry and speak up! We also goal to make more features such as minigames.

What can you do in Elf?

Elf enables you to make classes, Join in a class and chat, have personal talks with teacher, Share ideas and get closer with peers. Elf is literally a closed SNS, closed only for you and your classmates. Although Elf is originally for school, you can also use Elf in companies and other groups as well.
