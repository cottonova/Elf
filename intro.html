What is Elf?

Elf is a closed SNS for school. It was made for better communication between students and teachers, and furthermore between students. You can create classes, chat with teacher and students, and get closer with teachers. Elf will help you live a better school life. Outcasts, bullies, insiders, outsiders all can get together in Elf. Speak up with Elf!

What is SNS?

A social networking service (also social networking site, or SNS or social media) is an online platform which people use to build social networks or social relations with other people who share similar personal or career interests, activities, backgrounds or real-life connections.

Who use Elf?

Elf is made for students all over the world, and was made by Korean students. Elf's goal is to help all students get close with teachers. Eif is, and will be used all over the world.

What can you do in Elf?

Elf enables you to make classes, Join in a class and chat, have personal talks with teacher, Share ideas and get closer with peers.
